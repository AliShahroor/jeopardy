How to Play

🎮 Game Modes

  • Topic Mode: Choose 6 categories from 25+ built-in genres — from Science and History to Video Games, Anime, Football, and Flags of the World. The game builds a fresh, randomized board each time. Can't decide? Hit 🎲 Surprise Me to auto-pick 6 random categories.
  • Custom Mode: Write your own questions, answers, and use the built-in point values. Perfect for study sessions, parties, or themed games.

🎬 The Game Board

  • The board has 6 categories across the top and 5 rows of increasing point values ($200 to $1000).
  • The current player selects a cell to reveal a question.
  • A timer counts down - answer before time runs out!
  • After the answer is revealed, judge whether the response was correct or wrong.
  • Correct answers add points; wrong answers deduct points.
  • 💰 Steal: if a player gets it wrong, any other player can try to steal — answer correctly to take the points, or get it wrong and lose them. Or choose "No steal" to move on.
  • Play continues until all questions are answered.

⚡ Question Types

  • Text Questions: Standard trivia - read the question, discuss the answer, then reveal and judge.
  • Image Questions: Visual questions using emoji representations and descriptions.
  • Interactive Challenges: Rapid-fire rounds where a player must type as many answers as possible before the timer runs out (e.g., "Name 30 European countries in 60 seconds").

👥 Multiplayer

  • 2 to 4 players can compete.
  • Players take turns selecting questions from the board.
  • Any player can be selected to answer (use the player buttons in the question modal).
  • Wrong answers can be stolen by other players — so staying sharp on every question pays off.
  • The player with the highest score at the end wins!

💾 Save & Load

  • Click "Save" during a game to save your progress.
  • Access saved games from the main menu.
  • Games are saved per user using your browser's local storage.
  • You can save multiple games and continue them later.

🔗 Share With Friends

  • Click 🔗 Share during any game to get a link.
  • Anyone who opens the link gets your exact board — categories, questions, and timer — ready to play.
  • No account or sign-up needed for them to play your game.
  • Great for sharing a custom quiz you built for friends, a class, or a party.
